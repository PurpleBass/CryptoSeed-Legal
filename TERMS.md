2. Description of the App

CryptoSeed is a local-only cryptography utility for iOS that provides tools to encrypt and decrypt text and files, manage cryptographic keys, and perform related cryptographic operations entirely on device.

CryptoSeed includes:
	•	A user interface for encrypting and decrypting text and files
	•	Integration with Apple Shortcuts via App Intents
	•	Local management of symmetric keys and asymmetric identities

CryptoSeed:
	•	Does not require user accounts
	•	Does not operate servers
	•	Does not collect analytics or telemetry
	•	Does not transmit data unless the user explicitly chooses to share it

All cryptographic operations occur locally on the user’s device.
